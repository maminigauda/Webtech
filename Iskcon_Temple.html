Iskcon_Temple

  • In Pune, the first-ever ISKCON centre was established in 1984. Over time, many people started visiting, and the temple activities expanded. Thus, more space was needed for the temple. And in 1995, ISKCON guru HH Gopal Krishna Goswami Maharaj purchased the NVCC land of 6 acres to initiate the temple project.
